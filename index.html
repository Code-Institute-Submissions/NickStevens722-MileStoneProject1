Welcome!

Welcome to the Code Institute Golf Society!

"The Friendliest Golf Society in the South" - well at least all our members think so!

After every game we meet in the clubs bar for the prize giving, a meal and a drink or two. Plus of course the reliving of every shot and what might have been. The meal and a drink at the club is included in our green fee price.

​We have membership of over 180 active members with between 40 and 50 playing every week, this goes to prove that this is the society to join!

The yearly membership consists of the purchase of a society shirt. This is payable on the 1st June every year. ​We feel that having a society shirt is important in identifying that we are part of a well organised and professional golf society.

There are no big prizes just good friends playing golf at a reasonable price so why not join us every Thursday we know you will enjoy the day
