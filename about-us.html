About Us

The Torreblanca Golf Society is one of the oldest and most successful golf societies on the coast. But more ​importantly it is the friendliest.

​

The society was formed not only to play golf but for social activities. Our members are all friends (well sometimes), when we are not playing golf we meet regularly socially for theatre visits, BBQ,'s meals out etc.

We have a varied membership of lady and men players from many different countries.

​

Their handicaps range from 1 to 28/36.

We welcome new members and guests of all abilities. Whether you are based in Spain, the UK, or any other country. Guests are welcome providing there are tee times available. Members will always have priority when booking tee times.

​

We play different courses every Thursday so why not join us for a competitive but more importantly a friendly game of golf at competitive prices.

​

Every year we hold our own version of the four majors. The US masters, The US Open, The British Open and The PGA.

However the highlight of our year is our version of the Ryder Cup. Not Europe versus America but the North of England versus the South. This takes place in September each year when we travel away with our partners to a golf resort for four days.

This is one trip not to be missed.

Golf Society Rules

  1. The yearly membership consists of the purchase of a good quality society shirt. This is payable on the 1st June every year. We feel that a society shirt is important in identifying that we are part of a well organised and professional golf society.
  2. Members will receive the benefit of reduced green fees.
  3. Failure to pay your membership excludes you from the competition, order of merit points and reduced green fee.
  4. Competitions take place every Thursday and are stableford unless other wise stated.
  5. Maximum handicaps are 28 for men and 36 for ladies.
  6. The competition prizes are for 1st ,2nd, 3rd, in the case of a tie result will be decided on countback on the back nine. There are also prizes for nearest the pin in two on a par four and nearest the pin in one on a par three.
  7. Guests are welcome, providing there are tee times available, however they will pay more for their green fees, members will always have priority when booking tee times.
  8. There is a separate prize for guests, although guests are able to enter the nearest the pin in two on a par four and nearest the pin in one on a par three competition.
  9. New (unknown) members will be required to enter three cards before they receive a society handicap. During this time they will not be eligible to take part in the competition. They are able to enter the nearest the pin in two on a par four and nearest the pin in one on a par three competition.
  10. We only allow pick and place on the fairways.
  11. ​We DO NOT allow "Gimmies" or "Mulligans". All putts must go in the hole.
  12. When a ball lands on a cart path you are entitled to free relief, no nearer the hole, from either side of the path. (Local rules on cart paths to be observed)
  13. For Society games only you may without penalty, repair spike marks on the green before you putt, even if they are on the line of your putt.
  14. If you lose a hole through slow play you MUST ​invite the group behind to play through.
  15. For handicap adjustments see table below or ask our handicap secretary.

Handicap Changes

There is no adjustment for any player scoring 33 or 34 points.

​Any player scoring 32 points or below will receive .1 of a shot back.

When scoring 35 and over players will be cut according to their handicap:

Handicap 0 to 12 cut by .1

Handicap 13 to 18 cut by .2

Handicap 19 and above cut by .3

The winning player will automatically cut by 1 shot in addition to the above. Second will be cut by .5 and third by .3

The guest winner will also be cut by an additional .5.

Any cuts for general play will only be made for those who score over 43 plus points and will be decided by the committee.
